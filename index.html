4-Week Intensive Course Syllabus

Comprehensive training outline for The Villa Spa Academy

Week 1

Foundations of Esthetics

Introduction to skin anatomy, skin analysis techniques, customer consultations, hygiene standards, and salon sanitization processes.

Week 2

Manual Facial Treatments

Deep cleansing facials, manual extraction techniques, product knowledge (serums, toners, masks), and specialized massage techniques for skincare.

Week 3

Advanced therapies & Waxing

Full-body and facial waxing procedures, brow shaping, body scrubs, polish formulations, skin glow therapies, and sauna hygiene.

Week 4

Cosmetic Enhancements & Business Setup

Teeth whitening techniques, teeth gemming/polishing, client management, spa business startup guide, and social media branding strategies.
