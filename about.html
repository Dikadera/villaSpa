About The Villa Spa

Awka's sanctuary for holistic wellness, premium esthetics, and elite professional training led by Stephanie Jefia.

Since Day One

Our Story

The Villa Spa was founded in the heart of Awka as a response to the growing need for high-end, dedicated, and strictly professional spa services. We set out to redefine what a wellness lounge should be, combining elements of deep relaxation with advanced esthetic care.

Today, we are recognized as Anambra’s premier wellness destination, housing an state-of-the-art training academy alongside our luxury treatment chambers. Every treatment is designed as a custom investment in your confidence and health.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class clinical esthetic procedures and soothing holistic therapies that restore skin vitality, relieve stress, and elevate personal confidence.

Our Vision

To remain the benchmark of aesthetic excellence in Nigeria, bridging the gap between raw pampering and clinical results while training the next generation of industry leaders.

Founder & CEO
The Visionary

Meet Stephanie Jefia

"True beauty begins when you step out of the rush and make space for your soul to breathe."

Stephanie Jefia, the powerhouse behind The Villa Spa, is an accredited Esthetics Educator and skincare pioneer. Dedicated to the science of dermal health and the art of luxury relaxation, she established the TVS Training Academy to empower aspiring therapists with formal, hands-on certification.

Under her guidance, the academy has graduated dozens of beauty professionals who now operate successful clinics across West Africa. Her philosophy centers on safe, highly effective techniques combined with genuine African hospitality.
