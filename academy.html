TVS Training Academy

Launch your high-end esthetics career under Nigeria's elite practitioners. Certified, hands-on, and industry-ready.

Career Path

Professional Spa Training

Our **4-Week Intensive Spa & Esthetics Bootcamp** is a rigorous program designed to turn passionate students into certified, independent wellness therapists. We combine high-yield dermal theories with extensive, daily hands-on practice in our fully simulated spa ward.

Taught directly by CEO Stephanie Jefia and Guest Physicians, this curriculum covers both luxury spa treatments and complex medical esthetic processes. Graduating students acquire standard licensing support, access to starter supply packages, and placement recommendations.

High-Yield Program

Course Curriculum

Module 01

Dermal Science & Analysis

Melanin-rich skin histology, skin typing, diagnostic extractions, sanitation, and chemical formulation guidelines.

Module 02

Spa Body Therapies

Anatomy fundamentals, Swedish massage techniques, aromatherapy, body scrub compounding, and Hammam scrubbing procedures.

Module 03

Clinical Esthetics

Advanced chemical peels application, microneedling handling, hygiene control, and client preparation guidelines.

Module 04

Spa Business Setup

Client acquisition methods, service pricing strategies, social media branding, safety compliance, and business registration.

Our certificate carries national prestige and is highly respected by luxury spas and beauty salons across Nigeria, giving you a competitive edge from day one.

Academy FAQ

Do I need prior medical experience?

No, our curriculum starts from the absolute basics of skin biology and progresses into advanced esthetics, making it suitable for beginners.

Are installment payment plans available?

Yes, we accept up to 2 installment payments. A 60% deposit is required to secure your seat, and the remaining 40% is due by the second week.

Do you provide placement support?

We offer top-performing students immediate internships at The Villa Spa, as well as recommendations to partner salons across Anambra state.
