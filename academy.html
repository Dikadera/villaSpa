What It Feels Like to Train Here

There is a version of esthetics training common in Nigeria—a projector, a binder, and a mannequin. At TVS, you train inside a live, fully-functioning clinical spa under professional standards.

01

Theory First

You study the anatomy, melanocyte histology, and deep chemistry. Knowing *why* separates an elite practitioner from a basic technician.

02

Hands-On Models

The clinical tools you use are the same ones used on patients. Work on real models under close instruction, not plastic simulators.

03

Active Practitioners

Your instructors perform these treatments daily on high-end clients. They teach from live clinical updates, not static books.

04

Self-Paced Competency

If you need extra sessions on cannulation or lip mapping, you get them. You graduate when you are fully competent, not when a calendar expires.

FAQ

Common Questions

Find answers to quick questions regarding enrollment, timelines, and credentials.

Do I need prior medical experience to enroll?

No. Our beginner-friendly programs (Module 01, Facials, Spa & Wellness) are crafted for students starting from scratch. For advanced medical injectables (Botox, Fillers, and PDO Threads), a medical background (Doctor, Nurse, or related) is highly recommended for safety compliance.

Are installment payment plans available?

Yes. To secure your enrollment slot, a 60% deposit is required. The remaining 40% balance can be split and paid before the completion of your second week of classes.

Do you provide models and startup kits?

Yes. TVS Academy arranges real models for practical clinical days so you gain live experience. You will also receive a custom starter toolkit with sanitation gear, basic extractors, and study guides.

Is the certificate recognized?

Yes. TVS Clinical Academy graduates receive a Certificate of Attendance aligned with professional aesthetics development standards. This carries great weight inside major luxury spa networks across Nigeria.
